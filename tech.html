Technology

PE: Printable electronics (PE) is a new advanced manufacturing technology that enables lower-cost fabrication for the large-volume production of electronic devices. PE is a promising, environmentally friendly alternative to producing display or energy products at a low cost and with new possibilities like flexible devices.

THz technology: THz waves are located between microwave and infrared light. The unique properties of THz enable non-destructive inspection of quality control.

Patterns: QCBs are made from metamaterials and print simultaneously with the main printed devices. QCBs can be designed to detect specific features such as ink conductivity, spreading, and thickness.
